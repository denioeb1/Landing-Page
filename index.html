About Us

At Hikarii, we believe plushies are more than just cute companions. They are little sparks of happiness. Whether they arrive as a gift, brighten your day in a package or sit by your side, our plushies are made to uplift and connect people of all ages. Each plushie is crafted with care using high quality materials to ensure they are soft, durable and made to last. Our goal is simple: to bring every plushie to its rightful owner in a way that is safe, loving and full of joy. Hikarii is not just about collecting cute things it is about spreading warmth, comfort and smiles one plushie at a time.
